
The results from the given simulations can be found in table
\cref{table:results}, and shows the number of collisions, the total length of
the solution trajectories, and the number of branches added to each search tree
over a total of hundred simulation runs for each column. It is seen that the
\rrtfunnel{} algorithm performs better in terms of robustness up to and
including its uncertainty bound of \( 3 \) \IEEEunit{m/s}, while the benchmark
planner does collide in the same environment, with the same uncertainty.
